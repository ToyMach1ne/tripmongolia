A peaceful horseback riding adventure in Orkhon Valley

  • A peaceful horseback riding adventure in Orkhon Valley

  • Map
  • Photos
M-101
Tour Code
15 days/14 nights
Duration
  • Adventure
  • Horseback riding

The best way to experience real Mongolia is obviously on the back of a horse, because that is how the locals travel. As we ride through Orkhon Valley, you will cross small streams, encounter beautiful landscapes and admire vast valley from a real nomadic perspective. Some say the untouched landscape and unchanged nomadic tradition are the main attraction of the horseback riding tour in this historic site.

As you can appreciate this horseback tour is totally different from just renting a horse in ranch and horseback riding in the paved roads close to the city. A horseback riding adventure in historic Orkhon Valley will take you back in ancient time and make you feel as if you were the first to explore this wild yet peaceful land of ever changing valleys, hills and plains

INTERESTING SITES Ugii lake, Erdenezuu monastery, Tuvkhun monastery, Elsen tasarkhai,Hustai National Park
1

Day 1: Ulaanbaatar

Arrive the Ulaanbaatar and transfer to the hotel. After lunch, visit Natural History Museum. Not only complete Dinosaur skeletons but the World’s best collection of dinosaur eggs and their nests are on display. Overnight in a hotel.

ACCOMMODATION: Hotel

2

Day 2: Ulaanbaatar - Ugii Lake

This morning we head toward the to Lake Ugii. Ugii Lake is a wonderful place for birdlife – cranes and ducks, to name just a few species, migrate to the area around late April.
ACCOMMODATION: Tour camp
3

Day 3: Ugii Lake

Today you will get acquainted with your horse and tack and set out for the first ride. Later, we will drive to Khoshoo Tsaidam, and exploring ruins from the time of the Tureg Empire.

ACCOMMODATION: Tent camping
4

Day 4: Five trees

Ride towards Karakorum.  You may wish to spend some time for rest and trekking.

ACCOMMODATION: Tent Camping
5

Day 5: Karakorum

Today we will reach Karakorum, the ancient capital of Mongolia from where Chinggis Khaan launched his cavalry. There is a small town now. On arrival the first and most obvious landmark is the vast wall of Erdenezuu Monastery. The monastery was built in 1586 and is surrounded by a massive 400m x 400m wall, Which was built without any nail in the construction of the ornate temples, only a few of which remain standing after the communist purges. We will visit the monastery and then ride along the Orkhon River, where we will camp for the night.

ACCOMMODATION: Tent Camping
6

Day 6: Nariin Valley

We will continue riding until we reach Nariin Valley, which is on the way to Tuvkhun Monastery. We will camp in the valley tonight.

ACCOMMODATION: Tent Camping
7

Day 7-8: Tuvkhun Monastery

Today we will visit Tuvkhun Monastery, which is located on a rocky mountain hill surrounded by forests elevated in 2300 meters above sea level.

ACCOMMODATION: Tent Camping
8

Day 9-10: Orkhon Waterfall

Continue to Orkhon Waterfall. The waterfall is situated in the Orkhon Valley and this valley is known as the cradle of the Mongolian civilization since many of Mongolia’s ancient monuments lie within its borders.

ACCOMMODATION: Tent Camping
9

Day 11: Orkhon Waterfall

We will spend a day of leisure by the waterfall.

ACCOMMODATION: Tour Camp
10

Day 12: Elsen tasarkhai

Today we will drive to Sand Stretch Elsen tasarkhai , a unique combination of forested mountains and desert-type landscape in the same location. On arrival we will set up camp in the foothills of the Khogno Khan Mountain. This mountain is perfect for some afternoon hiking and there are some remains of an ancient monastery that should be explored.

ACCOMMODATION: Tent Camping
11

Day 13: Hustai National Park

After early breakfast drive to Khustai National Park to enjoy taking photos of the wild horse Takhi and other name is Prjewalskii horse.

ACCOMMODATION: Tour camp
12

Day 14: Ulaanbaatar

We start heading back to Ulaanbaatar. Transfer to your hotel. Visit Bogd Khan’s Palace Museum and Buddha Park. Enjoy a traditional folk concert and farewell dinner.

ACCOMMODATION: Hotel
13

Day 15: Departure

Transfer to the airport after breakfast.
